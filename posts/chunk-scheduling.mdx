How to schedule events on calendar without getting overwhelmed

Schedule blocks of time to focus on big tasks of categories. Like 2 hour block for chores, 3 hours for movies, 5 hours for yard work, instead of 15 minutes for taking out trash, doing dishes, cleaning bathroom, etc.
