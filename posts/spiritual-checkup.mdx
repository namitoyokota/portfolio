> Jesus replied: “‘Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind.’ This is the first and greatest commandment. And the second is like it: ‘Love your neighbor as yourself.‘ All the Law and the Prophets hang on these two commandments.” - Matthew 22:37:40 NIV

## Love The Lord

1. How is my personal relationship with God?
    - Am I spending time reading?
    - Am I spending time praying?
    - Am I spending time meditating (listening)?
    - Do I have a growing desire to spend more time with God?
    - Am I obeying God?
    - Am I aware of the sins in my life?
2. Am I serving God?
    - Am I serving the church?
    - Am I regularly praying for the church?
    - Am I welcoming others to the church?
    - Am I donating to the church?
    - Am I teaching children and the youth?
    - Am I doing my job with excellence?

## Love Your Neighbor

1. Am I loving my neighbors?
2. Am I spreading the Word?
3. Am I living in humility?
4. Am I forgiving others?
    - Your wife? Your husband?
    - Your parents?
    - Your children?
    - Your family?
    - Your friends?
    - Your coworkers?
